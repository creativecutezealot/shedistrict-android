1. Be Unapologetic


SheDistrict allows women to connect with other women from all walks of life. From stay at home moms to trophy wives and college girls to forever princesses -- SheDistrict is dedicated to being a space for all types of hobbies, interests and lifestyle. No judgement allowed.


2. Set Healthy Boundaries


The reason that SheDistrict is for women only is so that women have a space where they can feel safe to make friends and be social. Setting healthy boundaries is crucieal for any friendship to last.


3. Trust and Loyalty


Friendships can’t prosper without trust and loyalty.You’re here either because you’d like to welcome more like-minded women into your circle or you agree that sometimes it is hard to make friends, so SheDistrict is an option for you. Regardles of why you’ve joined us, it is important that any woman you decide to become friends with knows that she can trust you and that your loylaty is to your friendship. Girl Power!


4. Commitment and Consistency


You are all here for the same goal: to make friends and connections with those whose beliefs and values align with yours. It is important to be commited to creating and building your friendships and consictency is key in maintaining these friendships as well. SheDistrict encourages it’s members to get offline and be friends in person!


5. Respect Each Other


The best thing about SheDistrict is the freedom to choose. SheDistrict’s goal is to alow you to choose who you want to be friends with and who you want to associate with. SheDistrict has zero tolerance for abuse or harassment. We’ve made it so that you have access to the filters that allow you to show or hide profiles.


6. Have Fun!


We’re all here to have a good time, make connections, and make long term friendships.
